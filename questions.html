  10. Prompt: Please explain - to a non-technical person - the difference between server side and client side rendering. Be sure to include how templates and AJAX relate to the different ways to render content.

    Answer:

    Server-Side Rendering (SSR): It's like ordering a complete meal at a restaurant, and it's served to you as is. You don't get to customize it.
    Client-Side Rendering (CSR): It's like going to a buffet where you choose what dishes to put on your plate and how you arrange them. You have more control and can change things on the go.
    Templates: Think of them as cooking recipes. In SSR, the chef follows a recipe to make your meal. In CSR, you have ingredients (components) and can create your own dish.
    AJAX: It's like a waiter who brings you more dishes (data) while you're already eating without disrupting your meal (page). Both SSR and CSR can use this waiter to make the experience more dynamic.
